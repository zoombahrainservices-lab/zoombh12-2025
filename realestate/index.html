Commercial & Residential Leasing

Tailored leasing services

Our leasing services simplify this process, offering personalized leasing solutions for both residential and commercial properties that meet your specific needs.

  • Broad range of property options
  • Expertise in lease contract negotiations
  • Support for both tenants and landlords
  • Quick and transparent leasing process

Warehouse & Self Storage

Secure and flexible storage

Finding secure and flexible storage solutions can be difficult, especially for growing businesses with fluctuating inventory needs.

Our warehouse and self-storage services offer both short-term and long-term options, ensuring that your storage needs are met without hassle.

  • High-security storage facilities
  • Flexible rental terms
  • Multiple storage size options
  • Easily accessible locations

Lease Drafting & Preparation

Legally sound lease agreements

Drafting clear and fair lease agreements can be complicated and time-consuming, leading to potential disputes between tenants and landlords.

We provide professional legal drafting services that ensure both tenants and landlords have transparent and fair leasing contracts.

  • Professional, legally binding contracts
  • Clear terms and conditions
  • Customizable lease terms to fit your needs
  • Experienced legal team support

Property Maintenance Management

Complete property care

Managing property maintenance can be time-consuming and often results in tenant dissatisfaction when done poorly.

We offer comprehensive property maintenance services, ensuring your properties stay in excellent condition, keeping tenants satisfied and owners worry-free.

  • Scheduled and on-call maintenance
  • Highly qualified maintenance teams
  • Improved tenant satisfaction
  • Cost-efficient, proactive maintenance solutions

Full-service rental management

Our sole lettings agency service handles every aspect of property rental, from marketing to tenant management, ensuring your property is always generating income.

  • End-to-end rental property management
  • Tenant sourcing and screening
  • Full lease management and tenant support
  • Maximize rental income with minimal effort

Rent Collection

Hassle-free rent collection

We provide reliable rent collection services, ensuring on-time payments from tenants and hassle-free rental income for property owners.

  • Automated rent collection system
  • Timely and consistent payments
  • Detailed income tracking
  • Tenant support for payment queries
